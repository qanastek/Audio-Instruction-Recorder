Research Study Information

You are invited to participate in a research study about voice recording and data collection. This study is conducted by researchers at the University of Avignon.

Purpose

The purpose of this research is to collect voice recordings that will be used for academic research and will be publicly released online.

Data Collection

We will collect:

  • Your voice recordings
  • Your username
  • Recording timestamps
Data Usage

Your recordings will be:

  • Used for research purposes
  • Made publicly available online
  • Potentially used in future research studies
Rights and Participation

Your participation is voluntary. You can:

  • Choose not to participate
  • Stop participating at any time
  • Request the removal of your data by contacting us at contact@example.com
